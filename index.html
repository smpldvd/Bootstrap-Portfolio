Fundamentals

Like with most necessities, our philosophy was "if you can't find what you're looking for, make it!" Our app stems from a lack of certain features involed with keeping track of things of value we buy. We wanted an app that could track purchases and attach data, such as receipts, warranty, and location, as well as be able tag items with a unique QR code.

Log

Log items and attach receipts, images, and other pertinent data to it.

Manage

The app is capable of sorting through items and arranging it by categories.

Track

Have the abilty to print tags for item(s) stored in boxes and geotag its location.
